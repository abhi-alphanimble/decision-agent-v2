# Decision Agent for Zoho CRM

**Turn Team Conversations into Actionable Decisions**

---

## Overview

Decision Agent is a Slack-to-Zoho CRM integration that helps teams make and track decisions. Propose ideas in Slack, let your team vote, and automatically record every outcome in Zoho CRM.

No more lost decisions. No more forgotten agreements. Just clear, documented outcomes.

---

## How It Works

1. **Propose** — Any team member suggests a decision in Slack
2. **Vote** — The team votes to approve or reject
3. **Record** — Finalized decisions sync automatically to Zoho CRM

---

## Key Features

- **Democratic Voting** — Team members vote directly in Slack
- **Automatic Sync** — Every decision is recorded in Zoho CRM
- **AI Summaries** — Get quick summaries of complex proposals
- **Configurable Thresholds** — Set approval percentages per channel
- **Search History** — Find past decisions instantly
