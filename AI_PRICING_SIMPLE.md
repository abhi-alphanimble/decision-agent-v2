# AI Pricing - Quick Reference
**Model:** Google Gemini 2.5 Flash Lite via OpenRouter

---

## Pricing
- **Input:** $0.10 per Million tokens
- **Output:** $0.40 per Million tokens

## Monthly Cost Calculation

**Using average: 4,175 tokens/day per organization**

### Per Organization
- Daily: 4,175 tokens
- Monthly: 4,175 × 30 = **125,250 tokens**
- Monthly Cost: **$0.025** (roughly $0.03)

### 10 Organizations
- Monthly: 125,250 × 10 = 1,252,500 tokens
- **Monthly Cost: $0.25** (roughly $0.25-$0.30)
- **Annual Cost: $3.00**

### 15 Organizations
- Monthly: 125,250 × 15 = 1,878,750 tokens
- **Monthly Cost: $0.38** (roughly $0.40)
- **Annual Cost: $4.50**

### 20 Organizations
- Monthly: 125,250 × 20 = 2,505,000 tokens
- **Monthly Cost: $0.50** (roughly $0.50-$0.60)
- **Annual Cost: $6.00**

**For 10-20 organizations, expect to pay approximately:**
### **$0.30 to $0.60 per month** for all AI features
### **$3.60 to $7.20 per year** for all AI features

This is negligible compared to infrastructure costs and can easily be covered by a $5-10/month subscription tier.
